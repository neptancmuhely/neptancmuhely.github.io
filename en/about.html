Education is carried out using the world-famous Timár-method, the essence of which is to introduce as many children as possible to the still-living tradition of folk dance and folk music at an early age, and to be integrated into their life as a lived experience.

We would like to ensure that our dancers love and enjoy the beauty of folk dance, and that they come to our classes with joy and in a good mood.
We offer groups for children and youth from beginner to advanced level.

In our folklore show, we present the most beautiful dances of the Hungarian-speaking regions, with special emphasis on original and stylish performance.

The ensemble is led by Orsolya Mayer, László Németh-award winner folk dance teacher.
The ensemble consists of groups for children and youth.
