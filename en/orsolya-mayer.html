Orsolya Mayer
 Art Director

I am the Founder and Art Director of the Mayer Folk Dance Workshop. 10 years ago I started my own dance group as Bicskei Csillagszeműek, which now continues to operate as Mayer Folk Dance Workshop.
 
My goal for those who want to dance with us is to fall in love with and enjoy the beauty of dance, and to come to our classes with joy and in a good mood.

25 years of professional work:

I started my folk dancing career in 1993 in Sándor Timár's Corvin Dance Ensemble, which was the youth group of the Hungarian State Folk Ensemble.

1995-2001: I was a founding member and solo dancer at the Timár Chamber Dance Group.
1997-2021: I gained pedagogical experience as the leading teacher of the Csillagszemű Dance Ensemble and the Timár Academy.

My professional studies and awards: 
- Hungarian Academy of Dance, folk dance pedagogy
- László Németh-award, 2015
